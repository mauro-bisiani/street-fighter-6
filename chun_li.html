Commands

Special Moves

Kikoken

L(Hold), R + P 2

Fire a Ki blast directly forward.

Used for attacking opponents at range.

Hundred Lightning Kicks

D, DR, R + K 2

Unleash a series of high-speed kicks.

Useful for creating combos out of normal attacks.

Lightning Kick Barrage

K, K after an OD Hundred Lightning Kicks

A series of kicks that can be performed following an OD Hundred Lightning Kicks.

Useful for tacking on additional damage while also knocking down your opponent.

Aerial Hundred Lighting Kicks

D, DR, R + K during a neutral or forward jump 2

A Hundred Lightning Kicks performed while jumping.

Effective when used to convert a jumping attack into a combo.

Spinning Bird Kick

D(Hold), U + K 2

A series of rotating, upside-down kicks with your legs extended wide.

This move does a lot of damage, and is useful when performing combos against opponents that have left themselves wide open.

Hazanshu

D, DL, L + K 2

Leap forward, rotate in the air, and slam your leg downward with a powerful kick.

Useful for checking an opponent’s sweeps, or as a way to close distance.

Tensho Kicks

D, D + K 2

Rise into the air with multiple kicks.

Useful for countering an opponent’s jumping attacks, or as a way to close out combos.

Super Arts

Kikosho

D, DR, R, D, DR, R + P 1

Create a mass of energy directly in front of you.

Its swift speed and invulnerability can quickly turn the tables on an opponent.

Aerial Kikosho

D, DR, R, D, DR, R + P during a jump 1

Fire a kikosho diagonally downward while jumping.

Effective when used to convert a jumping attack into a combo.

Hoyoku-sen

D, DR, R, D, DR, R + K 2

Move forward while unleashing a series of kicks and then launch your opponent into the air. The last hit can be immediately canceled into a jump.

Soten Ranka

D, DL, L, D, DL, L + K 3

Perform a roundhouse kick, knock your opponent into the air with a series of attacks, and then smash them with a devastating kick.

Useful for tacking on big damage in a combo, or as a quick way to turn the tables.

Yokusen Kick

Falling Crane

A knee strike performed after placing yourself behind your opponent while leaping into the air.

Yoso Kick

D + MK during a jump-can be performed up to three times

A stomping attack performed while jumping. Follow-ups can be performed after the first hit connects with or is blocked by the opponent.

Wall Jump

While jumping, kick off a wall to perform another jump.

Soaring Eagle Punches

HP > HP during a jump

A series of attacks performed while jumping.

Serenity Stream

D, DL, L + P

Adopt a very low, stationary fighting stance. While in this stance, each attack button produces different attacks.

Orchid Palm

Snake Strike

A forward-moving, repeatable low profile strike, performed during Serenity Stream.

Lotus Fist

HP

An overhead attack that cannot be blocked while crouching, performed during Serentity Stream.

Forward Strike

A quick, low kick, performed during Serenity Stream.

Senpu Kick

A low kick with long reach, performed during Serenity Stream.

Tenku Kick

Throws

Koshuto

N or R + LP LK when near opponent

Seize your opponent by the arm, spin them around you, and throw them aside.

Throws your opponent without switching places with them.

Taiji Fan

L + LP LK when near opponent

Disrupt your opponent’s balance with an elbow strike, scoop them up, and toss them behind you.

Throws your opponent while switching places with them.

Ryuseiraku

LP LK during a jump-when near opponent

Catch your opponent in mid-air and slam them into the ground.

Throws your opponent whithout switching places with them.

Common Moves

Drive Reversal: Hoyoku Kick

HP HK 1

Lunge forward with a powerful attack.

Can be used to absorb an opponent’s attack while launching your own offensive.

Drive Impact: Hosen Kick

R + HP HK when blocking, recovering from a knockdown, or during a successful Drive Parry 2

Can be used after blocking an attack to quickly turn a defensive position into an offensive one. Can also be used when recovering from a knockdown. The input can be held while downed to perform this when rising.

Drive Parry

MP MK hold the button to extend the move’s duration >>

Adopt a stance that allows you to deflect all opponent strikes.

This stance can be sustained by holding down the command used to activate it.

Parry Drive Rush

R, R 0.5

Immediately move forward from a Drive Parry stance.

Normal or unique attacks performed while moving will have their properties changed.

Cancel Drive Rush

R, R or (N or R) + MP MK while connecting with a special-cancelable move 3

Immediately move forward out of an attack.

Can be performed during any special-cancelable move.
